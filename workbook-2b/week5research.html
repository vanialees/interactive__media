week 5 research
In week 5, we learned how to add a webcam feature into our p5 page and adding filter into it to make it appear more interactive. One of the activities that we did was making the webcam move as our cursor moved.

After the activity, I tried to create something more interactive at the same time taking inspiration from some of the websites I have looked at. We were assigned to find up to 10 activities by chance. Immediately as we were briefed about this task, I thought about being completely controlled by AI.

For example, using a TikTok filter to decide my outfit for the day, asking chatGPT what I should eat, and letting chatGPT control my day. Through this activity, it revealed numerous limitation even though I initially thought it would help decision making easier because I am getting assistance. However, midway while doing it, I realized that AI only knows some things about me. One take from doing this activity is that, for some instances, it made me ask chatGPT to give me another recommendation and it made me think whether or not I am doing this activity fairly (even though there is no fair or not fair).
